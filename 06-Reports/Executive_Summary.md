## Executive Summary

This project established a baseline ISO 27001-aligned compliance and risk management program for Sofunny101Cloud AI Solutions.

### Findings
- 15 security controls evaluated
- 6 fully implemented
- 7 partially implemented
- 2 planned

### Key Risks
- Lack of MFA enforcement
- Incomplete logging and monitoring
- Inconsistent access reviews

### 90-Day Improvement Plan
- Enforce MFA for all users
- Centralize logging into SIEM
- Conduct quarterly access reviews
- Formalize incident response testing
